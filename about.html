All about me:


Hi everyone, and welcome to my page. My name is Jonathan (but most people call me Jona) and I love Malaysia. From the food, to the people, to the natural beauty, I think Malaysia is an amazing country. My goal with this site is to learn to speak Bahasa Malaysia, and I hope some of my readers can use it as a resource toward learning it as well. I'll also add some authentic Malaysian recipes to the page, using mostly Malay for the ingredients, because I think the best way to learn about language and culture is through your stomach.

I hope you enjoy reading this site as much as I've enjoyed making it!
